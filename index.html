Rock Paper Scissors Game

Welcome! Here's how to play:

  • Right click anywhere on the screen, then click "inspect"
  • Click "console" in the top menu bar
  • Click in the empty space and type "game()" then hit enter.
  • Play the game in the console. Enter the same "game()" command to play again, or enter "tieBreaker()" to play a single round that repeats in the event of a tie.
